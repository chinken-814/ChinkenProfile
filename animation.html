Chinken is shy!

Because she is living inside the forest, it is almost impossible to meet with someone new. Therefore, if she sees someone near her home, she will hide in some tail grass and check if they are harmless. Also, she stammer when she is talking with someone new. Expect those, she acts like a normal person.
