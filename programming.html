Chinken know magic!

She is a witch but not very well-trained. She can only use a spell that summons 30 frogs at a time, as army with her bamboo flute. The frogs will attack the one who tries to invade her home. Most of the time, she just summons them for fun. Those frogs can be seen as pets and seems they love to follow her.
