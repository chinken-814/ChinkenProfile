Welcome to Witch's House!

Chinken existed in the Middle Ages where people were still fighting with swords and bows. It is special that there is not any living creature that looks like Chinken. Therefore, people will be scared if they see her.

The Witch's House is a house that stand alone deep down in a forest. Inside of it, you can find many broke wands that were burned or broke in half. You can also found some potions that with a no good looking color. Beside the house, there is a little farm that where Chinken plant her food and keep her little frogs safe.

Its surroundings are usually very foggy. Therefore, you can only find that place if you are invited or very lost.
